Fried Eggs

Fried eggs are one of my favorite things to make for breakfast. It's very easy to make and if you do it like I do it won't take much time. I personally like my fried eggs crispy like in the image above, so this recipe will show you how to acheive that.

Ingredients

  • An egg (or as many as your pan can hold)
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • A pan
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in the pan until it's just about to smoke.
  2. Crack your egg into the hot oil.
  3. Tilt your pan so that the oil pools in the side.
  4. Use a spoon to baste the egg in the hot oil
  5. Take the egg out and plate it once you reach your desired doneness.
