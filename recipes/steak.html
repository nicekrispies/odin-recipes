Pan Seared Steak

Ingredients

  • A steak of your choice
  • A high smoke point oil
  • Meat thermometer
  • Salt & pepper
  • Butter, garlic, and thyme (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a cast iron or pan until it's just about to smoke.
  2. Add the steak making sure that you are laying it away from yourself.
  3. Let the steak sit on one side for 3 to 4 minutes before flipping.
  4. Flip the steak and lower the heat if you're adding aromatics and butter.
  5. Optional: Add butter and aromatics. Use a spoon to baste the steak in the melted butter.
  6. Stick your meat thermometer in sideways and cook until you steak is about 5 degrees out from your desired doneness.
  7. Take steak out of the pan and let it rest for a few minutes.
